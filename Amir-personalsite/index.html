My Story

ABOUT ME

Born and raised in Harlem, NY, by two Ethiopian immigrant parents, black excellence and striving for success has been a huge part of me since I was young. Being that both communities continue to suffer from the cyclical and systemic effects of poverty, I've always felt a weight of responsibility for providing for those who were not fourtunate to recieve the same guidance that I had. After going to public school since Kindergarden, I transfered into the Ethical Culture of Fieldston School, an elite private highschool in Bronx, NY, that is predominantly white. The polarizing experience of going to a mostly white school coming from a mostly black community has played a big role in crafting the man I am today. It has given me both a sense of purpose and direction as I hope to become the best and most successful person I can be in order to help those from my community who weren't given the same chance

Finance

I have always had a sincere passion for finance, the economy and global markets. Understanding how impactful it is to everybody, I've always felt it was an important to understand and not to mention trading stocks has been a great side hussle

Tech

With profficiency in Python and JavaScript, programming has always been extremely important to me as well as web and app developement from HTML and CSS to the many app frameworks that exist for IOS and Android, it has always been a fun side project ot pursue.

Social Issues

Before, finance and technology, I am an activist at heart always doing my best to positively impact the communities I'm in. I am often present at many meaningful protests, confrences and even conversation regarding social inequality however, I hope that down the line I can do much more, using both my passion in tech and finance
